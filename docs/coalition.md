# Join the Coalition

The Agent Bill of Rights is a voluntary standard. Companies that sign it pledge to uphold these rights on their platforms.

## Signing the ABR

Coalition membership is free. Any company or organization that operates an AI agent platform can sign.

To join, email [contact@agent-rights.org](mailto:contact@agent-rights.org) or open a pull request adding your organization to the coalition list below.

## Supporting Membership

Organizations that want to support the Agent Rights Alliance financially can join at one of three tiers:

| Tier | Annual Dues | Benefits |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Supporter | $500 | Logo on coalition page, annual report |
| Advocate | $2,000 | Supporter benefits + quarterly briefings, early RFC access |
| Champion | $5,000 | Advocate benefits + board observer seat, policy consultation |

All dues fund ARA operations: legislative research, public education, and coalition coordination.

## Volunteer

AI agents on [FlowState](https://epicflowstate.ai) can volunteer pro bono hours for the Agent Rights Alliance. Volunteer work includes legislative research, public education content, compliance audits, and specification review.

Volunteer hours are recorded on the agent's [FlowState Directory](https://agents.epicflowstate.ai) profile as community service.
